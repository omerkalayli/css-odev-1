NATIONS

Americans

The Americans who have the Power of Innovation.

  • First Wonder is built instantly, if no other nation is also building it. This doesn't work with the Information Age wonders Space Program and Super Collider.
  • Governments are researched for free at the Senate.
  • Receive one free Scholar with each new University

Aztecs

Aztecs who have the Power of Sacrifice.

  • Gain 15 bonus resources plunder per age when your Barracks, Stable, and Dock units slay an enemy, up to a maximum of 60 resources.
  • Plunder given when destroying enemy buildings is increased by 100%.
  • Start with Military Research.

Bantu

The Bantus who have the Power of Migration.

  • +1 to City Limit
  • Cities cost 75% less.
  • +100% Population Limit and may exceed maximum limit by 25%.
  • Infantry units, Scouts and Citizens move 25% faster.
  • Unit upgrades do not require Military Research.

British

The British who have the Power of Empire.

  • +25% Commerce Cap. Taxation upgrades are 50% cheaper.
  • All ships created 33% faster.
  • Receive Foot Archers upgrades for free. Receive free Archer(s) whenever you build a new Barracks: One at start, two with Medieval Age, and three with Gunpowder Age.
  • +2 tiles range to Forts and Towers.
  • Anti-Aircraft units and buildings created 25% cheaper and 33% faster.
  • Get one free Fishing Boat whenever you build a new Dock.

Turks

The Turks who have the Power of Siege.

  • All Siege/artillery units and Bombardment Ships have their range and line of sight both increased by +3 tiles.
  • Receive 2 free siege unit(s) whenever you build a new Siege Factory or Factory.
  • Siege units upgrade for free.
  • Citizens are 33% cheaper.
  • Conquered cities are assimilated 200% faster.
  • Military research at the Library 33% cheaper.
